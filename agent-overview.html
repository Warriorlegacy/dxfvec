dxf-vectorizer

system · the job

Preprocesses the input image (OpenCV), extracts geometry (vision LLM via LiteLLM), generates a layered DXF (ezdxf), validates it, and writes review.md.

system · never-dos

Never guess dimensions · never mark a line BEND unless clearly dashed · never drop geometry silently · always validate DXF before finishing.

Outcome

user.define_outcome · max_iterations 3

Rubric graded by the platform — rubric.content in the kickoff event.

  1. drawing.dxf exists and ezdxf.readfile() succeeds
  2. At least one closed LWPOLYLINE on the CUT layer (part outline)
  3. All visible holes as CIRCLE entities on the CUT layer
  4. BEND layer present with fold lines OR review.md states "no bend lines detected"
  5. review.md exists with entity table, confidence, and ambiguities list
